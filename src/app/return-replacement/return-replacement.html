Return & Replacement Policy

At Your Store Name, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, we are here to help with easy returns and replacements.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items should be unused and in their original condition with packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice/order ID) is required.

Non-Returnable Items

  • E-books or digital products.
  • Items purchased under clearance or final sale.

Replacement Process

If your item qualifies for replacement, we will send a new product at no additional cost once the returned item is verified.

How to Request a Return

  1. Go to your account and navigate to My Orders.
  2. Select the order and choose Request Return/Replacement.
  3. Follow the instructions to schedule a pickup or ship the item back.
